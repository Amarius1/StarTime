Ariane 5

Payload to GTO: 10t
Payload to LEO: 20t
Mass: 780t
Height: 50.5m
search search search
This is the payload section of the Ariane ship. Fragile cargo like satellites and space probes are protected by the aerodynamic fairing by external forces while also making the rocket more stable during flight. Under it lies the second stage ESC-AH Engine that carries the payload into orbit.
The Ariane 5 fuel tank is what powers the main engine that pushes the whole rocket to space and it's the structure the boosters are attached to. At the bottom lies the EPC H 173 Cryogenic engine.
These are the two Ariane 5 Solid Rocket Boosters that help the rocket achieve its necessary speed and they are discarded once their fuel runs out, before the main stage detaches.
