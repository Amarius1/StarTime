Falcon 9 Block 5

Payload to GTO: 8.3t
Payload to LEO: 22.8t
Mass: 550t
Height: 71m
search search search
This is the payload section of the Falcon 9 rocket. While launching satellites it has Fairing around them to protect them from the aerodynamic forces. During ISS resupply flights it hosts the Dragon space craft that carries supplies and experiments to the station.
The Falcon 9 fuel tank is what fuels the 9 Merlin engines that help the rocket reach orbit. Attached at the top there are 4 titanium grid fins that help during the vertical landing procedures.
At the bottom of the main fuel tank of the Falcon 9 there are 4 legs attached that are deployed just before the landing procedure.
